EPUB & Manga Reader

Read EPUB books and manga in your browser — with light, dark and sepia themes and reading progress synced across your devices.

View on GitHub Privacy Policy

Features

  • Read EPUB books and manga directly in your browser.
  • Light, dark and sepia reading themes for comfortable reading, with a consistent, clean layout across books.
  • Efficient streaming and caching, built to handle very large EPUB files without consuming excessive memory.
  • Reading progress (chapter and page) is saved and synced across your devices via your own Google Drive.
  • Files are read locally from your computer — your books never leave your device.

Privacy

This extension does not collect personal information. The only data it handles is your reading progress, stored privately in your own Google Drive app-data folder. Read the full Privacy Policy.
